Vital Signs & Nursing Assessment

Blood Pressure Measurement

Purpose

Measures the force of blood against artery walls.

Equipment

Cuff and stethoscope or automated monitor

Who performs it?

Nurses, physicians and trained healthcare staff

Important point

Correct cuff size, resting position and repeated readings improve accuracy.

Equipment

Important point

About MedMinute

A small initiative with a clear purpose.

1 min estimatedLast updated July 15, 2026

MedMinute was created in Qatar to help students understand medicine and science without unnecessary complexity.

🎯

Mission

To make medicine, biology, and chemistry clearer and more accessible for students through simple explanations and educational media.

🔭

Vision

To encourage scientific curiosity and help students confidently explore future careers in healthcare.

Founder

Saad Aboumalik

Saad Aboumalik is a Grade 12 student at Blyth Academy Qatar and an aspiring physician passionate about medicine and science education. He founded MedMinute to make complex medical and scientific topics clear, accessible, and engaging for students while encouraging the next generation to explore careers in healthcare.

MedMinute is currently in development. New lessons, specialty pages, and video links will be added gradually.
